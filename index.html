Venezuela Shortage Crisis

How does a country with one of the largest reserve of oils in the world turn out into a starving nation?

Short Story by Sharay Pichardo

Around the world, the country of Venezuela was famous for its large oil reserves, beautiful women, stunning landscapes, agriculture and distinct natural regions. Today, Venezuela is known for its shortage on basic goods such as food, medicine, water, and electricity. It is also notorious for its hyperinflation, corrupt government, and highest crime rates in the world. According to the newspaper Independent, Caracas, Venezuela, was ranked # 1 as the most violent cities in the world.
